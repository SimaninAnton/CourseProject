SimpleCookie is used to generate the Set-cookie headers, but BaseCookie is used to parse them.
For example, if a '=' is included in the cookie value, SimpleCookie will quote the whole cookie value: Set-cookie: foo="bar". However, BaseCookie will read that value in as '"bar"' instead of 'bar'.
