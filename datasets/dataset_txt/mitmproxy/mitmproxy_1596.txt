I followed the guide on http://mitmproxy.org/doc/ssl.html , but the self generated PEM can't be used to generate the interception certificate on the fly. In browser, only the self generated cert is present.
I used the following command line to produce the above result:
mitmproxy --cert ca.pem
However, I replaced the ~/.mitmproxy/mitmproxy-ca.pem with my self-generated cert ca.pem and run using the following command:
mitmproxy
It works! Interception certificate is generated on-the-fly.
