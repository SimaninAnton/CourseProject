djangorestframework.views change the script_prefix.
But in my case, the orig_prefix is already right and the concatenation of orig_prefix and prefix make it looks like :
http://localhost:8000http://localhost:8000/api/ instead of http://localhost:8000/api/
Here is my patch but I am not quite sure in which case the orign_prefix can be wrong.

+++ b/djangorestframework/views.py  Tue Jan 03 14:32:03 2012 +0100
@@ -109,7 +109,8 @@
