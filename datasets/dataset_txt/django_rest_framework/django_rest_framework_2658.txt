I have some objects that have a lot of optional attributes, and the expectation is that the output JSON should just have the required and populated attributes.
There should be options to mute None-valued attributes in output.
At the serializer level
Applied to all contained fields
Option to specify at serializer declaration or instantiation.
Nested serializers could inherit or override active option
At the field level
Applied to values of the field
Option to specify at field declaration or instantiation.
At the renderer level
Option to specify at serializer declaration or instantiation.
In this case, instantiation-time would likely be most-used option
