get_params() returns build_fn as a parameter name which set_params() does not accept.
To reproduce:
from keras.wrappers.scikit_learn import KerasClassifier
from keras.models import Sequential
m = KerasClassifier(Sequential())
m.set_params(**m.get_params())
Raises:
ValueError: build_fn is not a legal parameter
