How this works for me, MWE:
cd ~
mkdir -p foo/bar/baz
touch foo/bar/baz/__init__.py

cd ~/foo/bar/baz
pipenv --three  # -> Pipfile: ~/foo/bar/baz/Pipfile

cd ~/foo/bar
pipenv --three  # -> Pipfile: ~/Pipfile
Why would the latter be the case?
