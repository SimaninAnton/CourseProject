Updating a dev package leads to it being added to the non-dev section. This is because ctx.invoke(install, ...) doesn't automatically forward the dev=True flag.
For instance, running pipenv update --dev mock in the pipenv repo causes mock to be moved from dev-packages to packages.
Best of all would be if the --dev flag wasn't necessary for update, it instead keeping packages in place.
