./letsencrypt-auto fails if VirtualHost contains FastCgiExternalServer
Sample virtualhost conf:
<VirtualHost *:80>
  ...
  DocumentRoot /srv/wwwroot/blog/htdocs
  Alias   /php5-fcgi   /usr/lib/cgi-bin/php5-fcgi-blog
  FastCgiExternalServer /usr/lib/cgi-bin/php5-fcgi-blog -socket /var/run/php5-fpm.blog.sock -pass-header Authorization
</VirtualHost>
Comment the FastCgiExternalServer part and letsencrypt-auto succeeds
Command line
./letsencrypt-auto --verbose --server https://example.com/ --webroot-path /srv/wwwroot/example/htdocs certonly
