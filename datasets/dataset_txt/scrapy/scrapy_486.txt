CSV export on Windows create unnecessary blank lines after each line.
You can fix the problem just by adding
newline=''
as parameter to io.TextIOWrapper in the init method of the CsvItemExporter class in scrapy.exporters
Details are over here:
https://stackoverflow.com/questions/39477662/scrapy-csv-file-has-uniform-empty-rows/43394566#43394566
