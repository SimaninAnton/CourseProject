As indicated at https://twistedmatrix.com/trac/browser/trunk/NEWS, a Twisted interface that had been deprecated was removed from the latest version:
v14.0.0
twisted.internet.interfaces.IStreamClientEndpointStringParser is now deprecated in favor of twisted.internet.interfaces.IStreamClientEndpointStringParserWithReactor. (#5069)
v15.5.0
twisted.internet.interfaces.IStreamClientEndpointStringParser has been removed and Twisted will no longer use parsers implementing this interface. (#8094)
