This is a simple IPython test case:
In [1]: from scrapy.selector import Selector

In [2]: Selector(text='<div x="It\x22s"', type="html")
Out[2]: <Selector xpath=None data=u'<html><body><div x="It" s></div></body><'>
The data in the selector shows <div x="It" s> instead of <div x="It\"s"> or <div x="It&quot;s">
This has been tested in Scrapy 1.2.2
