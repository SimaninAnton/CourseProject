An example from the command line on the difference between netloc and hostname.
import urlparse
urlparse.urlparse('http://www.iol.co.za:80/news/crime-courts/finally-perhaps-justice-for-anene-1.1598738').netloc
'www.iol.co.za:80'
urlparse.urlparse('http://www.iol.co.za:80/news/crime-courts/finally-perhaps-justice-for-anene-1.1598738').hostname
'www.iol.co.za'
According to the URI spec the port is not part of the hostname aka domain portion of the URL.
