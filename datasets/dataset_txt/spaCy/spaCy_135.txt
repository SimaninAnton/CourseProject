The tag "DET" is missing from the Portuguese tagmap and is mapped to "X" rather than "DET" (the word "no" in the following example).
>>> import spacy
>>> nlp = spacy.load('pt_core_news_sm')
>>> doc = nlp('em Damão e Diu e no estado de Goa')
>>> for token in doc:
 print(token.text, token.tag_, token.pos_)
