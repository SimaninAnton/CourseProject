The sklearn.linear_model.ARDRegression misses the attribute lambda_ that it should have according to the documentation. It's an easy fix, there seems to be "self.lambda_ = lambda_" missing at the end of the fit() function in line 429.

iris = sklearn.datasets.load_iris()
clf.fit(iris.data, iris.target)
