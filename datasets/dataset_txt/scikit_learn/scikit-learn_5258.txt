When choosing the alpha coefficient of a ridge regression with a custom loss function, like in:
        clf = linear_model.RidgeCV(alphas=[0.1, 1.0, 10.0], loss_func=mean_absolute_error)
the loss function is interpreted as score function (ie. maximized and not minimized).
As far as I understand, the problem lies in line 589 of ridge.py, that should be:
        estimator = _RidgeGCV(self.alphas, self.fit_intercept, self.normalize,
                             self.score_func, self.loss_func,
                              gcv_mode=self.gcv_mode,
                              store_cv_values=self.store_cv_values)
instead of:
        estimator = _RidgeGCV(self.alphas, self.fit_intercept,
                             self.score_func, self.loss_func,
                              gcv_mode=self.gcv_mode,
                              store_cv_values=self.store_cv_values)
(the self.normalize parameter is missing)
