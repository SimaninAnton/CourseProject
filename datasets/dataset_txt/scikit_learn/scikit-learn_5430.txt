If the samples in my dataset are grouped by class (e.g. y = [1, 1, 1, 1, ..., 2, 2, 2, 2, 2, ...]), KFold may produce completely useless folds. A shuffle option could be added to solve this problem. The main difference with ShuffleSplit is that the folds would be guaranteed to not overlap.
