I haven't dug in here to see if this is by design, but GradientBoosting*.estimators_ is an ndarray of 1d ndarrays containing DecisionTreeRegressors. The other tree ensemble methods have lists of the trees AFAICT. Leads to inconsistent indexing for gradient boosting vs. others. Admittedly, it might be more trouble to fix, if not intended, than to work around.
