The f1_score with arg average='micro' just gives the accuracy:
T = 1000
N = 10

preds = np.random.randint(0, N, size=T)
#preds = np.ones([T, ])
true = np.random.randint(0, N, size=T)

acc = (preds == true).mean()
print('acc={}'.format(acc))

f1 = f1_score(preds, true, average='micro')
print('f1={}'.format(f1))

np.isclose(acc, f1)
