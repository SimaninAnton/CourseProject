A little mistake in formula of MinMaxScaler, as shown in (https://github.com/scikit-learn/scikit-learn/blob/master/doc/modules/preprocessing.rst#scaling-features-to-a-range).
X_scaled = X_std / (max - min) + min 
/ should be replaced with *:
X_scaled = X_std * (max - min) + min
