The definition of adjusted mutual information says it should be a float between 0 and 1.0, yet when you score random multinomial labels you will often get negative values. For example:
y1 = [0,4,5,4,2,2]
y2 = [4,1,5,2,4,5]
print adjusted_mutual_info_score(y1,y2)
-0.153845866193
